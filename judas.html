Breakdown

My first industry experience. I interned at Ghost Story the summer after high school, working remotely with the team on environment art and level design.

I spent most of my time sourcing reference images and preparing them for legal review, hundreds of photos of industrial spaces, machinery, signage. I also designed a combat encounter layout and built 3D blockouts for the garage level computers.

Jonathan Loporto, my high school teacher, made this possible. Leonie, Lucas, Shawn, and Chad mentored me throughout. I learned more in those two months than I had in the past year of solo projects.

Key Accomplishments

  • Sourced and organized reference art for environment teams
  • Prepared assets for legal and copyright review
  • Designed and iterated on a combat encounter layout
  • Created 3D blockouts for garage level environment assets
