Breakdown

I worked at Ghost Story Games for the summer on environment art and level design.

I blocked out retro computers, desks, and miscellaneous props for the garage level in 3ds Max and Maya. I also designed a combat encounter using server racks as obstacles and water pipes as environmental hazards.

Leonie, Lucas, Shawn, and Chad mentored me throughout my time there. I learned how immersive sims actually get built, how spaces tell stories through the enviroment.

Jonathan Loporto made this opportunity happen. That summer changed how I approach game development.

Key Accomplishments

  • Created 3D blockouts for garage level props and furniture
  • Designed combat encounter using environmental hazards
  • Sourced and organized reference imagery for environment teams
  • Prepared assets for legal and copyright review
