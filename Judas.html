Project Overview

Judas is a roguelite where you play as the mysterious and troubled Judas. Your only hope for survival is to forge or betray alliances with your worst enemies. Will you work together to fix what you destroyed, or will you let it all burn?

What I Worked On

  • 3D blockouts of garage level computers.
  • Sourced references from artists for copyright approval.
  • Designed combat level.

Tech Used

Unreal Engine 4, Maya & 3ds Max

Challenges & Learnings

For this project, my biggest challenge was gaining confidence while working alongside industry professionals I deeply admire. It was intimidating at first, but I learned to trust my skills and contribute meaningfully to the team.
